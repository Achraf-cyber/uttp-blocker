UTTP Blocker - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

TL;DR: UTTP Blocker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Don't Collect

UTTP Blocker is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your personal information
  • Your YouTube viewing history
  • Your comments or interactions
  • Your browsing habits
  • Any data that could identify you

2. How the Extension Works

UTTP Blocker operates entirely on your device and only:

  • Reads YouTube comments to apply filtering rules locally
  • Stores your preferences (filter settings) locally in your browser
  • Fetches public blacklist data from our GitHub repository (no personal data involved)
  • Hides filtered comments from your view without transmitting any data

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Filter preferences: Stored locally in your browser
  • Blacklist data: Cached locally for performance
  • No external servers: No data is sent to external services

4. Permissions Used

UTTP Blocker requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your filter preferences locally
  • Alarms: To periodically update blacklist data
  • YouTube access: To read and filter comments on YouTube pages

5. Third-Party Services

The extension only connects to:

  • GitHub Pages: To fetch public blacklist data (no personal information involved)
  • YouTube: To access comment sections for filtering

6. Data Security

Since all data processing happens locally on your device:

  • No data transmission means no risk of data breaches
  • Your preferences are only accessible to you
  • No external parties can access your data

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and modify your stored preferences at any time
  • Uninstall the extension, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, please contact us:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/achraf-cyber/uttp_blocker
  • Homepage: https://achraf-cyber.github.io/uttp_blocker/

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and applies to all versions of the UTTP Blocker extension.
